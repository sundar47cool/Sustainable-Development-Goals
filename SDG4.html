SDG 4: Quality Education

Sustainable Development Goal 4 (SDG 4) is the education goal It aims to “ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all.” Education is the foundation for children, youth and entire communities to reach their full potential. While carrying out our programs, we have witnessed the power of education to change everything.



How can we Provide Quality Education to all ?

1) Using International Monetary Funds to invest and using the profits to start and run free schools across the world.
2) Using Idle funds of Central Banks of Developed Countries to Invest in AAA rated bonds and using the profits to start universities.
3) Reduce the pay of Governemnt school teachers by 20% and using that funds to start new schools.
